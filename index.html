Loyalty Shift Index

A Smarter Way to Measure Customer Loyalty Momentum

The Loyalty Shift Index (LSI) is a supplemental metric to Net Promoter Score (NPS) that quantifies the directional movement of customer sentiment over time. Unlike static NPS snapshots, LSI highlights the magnitude and quality of change, whether a customer is becoming more loyal, staying neutral, or declining in sentiment. This gives CX teams early warning signals and a sharper view into what actions are working, or not.
